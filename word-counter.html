📊 Word Count Limits — Alag Alag Platforms ke liye

Har platform ki alag word ya character limit hoti hai. Yeh common limits yaad rakhein:

  • Twitter / X post — 280 characters maximum
  • Instagram caption — 2,200 characters (lekin 125 se zyada chhupta hai)
  • Facebook post — 63,206 characters (practically unlimited)
  • Google meta description — 155-160 characters ideal
  • SEO blog post — minimum 800 words recommended
  • University essay (short) — usually 500 se 1,000 words
  • University thesis chapter — 5,000 se 10,000 words
  • WhatsApp message — 65,536 characters maximum

❓ Aksar Pooche Jane Wale Sawal — FAQ

Q: Kya yeh word counter bilkul free hai?
A: Haan, bilkul free hai. Koi account banana nahi, koi payment nahi, koi limit nahi. ToolsUrdu ke sab tools hamesha free rahenge — yeh hamara promise hai.
Q: Kya Urdu text bhi count hota hai?
A: Haan! Urdu, English, Roman Urdu — koi bhi language ka text paste karo, sab count hoga. Hamare counter mein koi language restriction nahi hai.
Q: Mera text safe hai? Kya koi dekh sakta hai?
A: Bilkul safe. Aapka text sirf aapke browser mein process hota hai — hamare server par kuch bhi send nahi hota. Aapka personal ya confidential text completely private rehta hai.
Q: Maximum kitne words count ho sakte hain?
A: Koi limit nahi — chahe hazar words hon ya lakh, sab count honge. Hamare tool mein text size ki koi restriction nahi.
Q: Word counter aur character counter mein kya farq hai?
A: Word counter sirf words count karta hai (space se alag alag lafz). Character counter har ek letter, number, space, aur punctuation mark count karta hai. Hamare tool mein dono ek saath milte hain!
Q: Reading time kaise calculate hota hai?
A: Average adult 238 words per minute padhta hai aur 130-140 words per minute bolta hai. Hamare tool mein yahi average use hota hai aur approximate reading/speaking time calculate hota hai.
Q: Kya yeh tool offline bhi kaam karta hai?
A: Page load hone ke baad text counting feature offline bhi kaam karta hai kyunki sab processing browser mein hoti hai. Lekin page dobara load karne ke liye internet chahiye.

BMI Calculator Kya Hai? — بی ایم آئی کیا ہوتا ہے؟

BMI yani Body Mass Index ek number hai jo aapke weight aur height ke ratio se calculate hota hai. Yeh batata hai ke aap apni height ke hisaab se healthy weight range mein hain ya nahi. Pakistan mein doctors aur nutritionists bhi yahi formula use karte hain.

BMI formula hai: Weight (kg) ÷ Height² (m²). Misaal ke taur par agar aapka weight 70kg hai aur height 170cm hai to BMI = 70 ÷ (1.70)² = 24.2 — jo normal range mein hai.

Q: Healthy BMI kitna hona chahiye?
A: 18.5 se 24.9 ke darmiyan BMI normal aur healthy maana jata hai. 25 se upar overweight aur 30 se upar obese category hai.
Q: Kya BMI Pakistani logon ke liye accurate hai?
A: WHO ke mutabiq Asian logon ke liye 23 se upar BMI overweight consider hota hai. Hamare tool mein standard international ranges use ki gayi hain.
Q: Agar BMI high ho to kya karein?
A: Apne doctor ya nutritionist se milein. Regular exercise aur balanced diet se weight manage karna mumkin hai.

BMI Calculator Kya Hai? — بی ایم آئی کیا ہوتا ہے؟

BMI yani Body Mass Index ek number hai jo aapke weight aur height ke ratio se calculate hota hai. Yeh batata hai ke aap apni height ke hisaab se healthy weight range mein hain ya nahi. Pakistan mein doctors, nutritionists aur fitness experts bhi yahi formula daily use karte hain.

BMI ka formula hai: Weight (kg) ÷ Height² (m²). Misaal ke taur par agar aapka weight 70kg hai aur height 170cm hai to BMI = 70 ÷ (1.70)² = 24.2 — jo bilkul normal aur healthy range mein hai. Hamare tool mein yeh sab calculation automatically hoti hai — sirf weight aur height daalo!

📊 BMI Ranges — Pakistan ke liye Complete Guide

World Health Organization (WHO) ke mutabiq BMI ranges yeh hain — aur Pakistan mein yahi standards follow hote hain:

  • 18.5 se kam — Underweight: Wazn kam hai, ghezia behtar karein
  • 18.5 se 24.9 — Normal/Healthy: Bilkul theek hai, aise hi rahein
  • 25.0 se 29.9 — Overweight: Wazn thoda zyada hai, diet pe dhyan dein
  • 30.0 se 34.9 — Obese Class I: Motapa hai, doctor se zaroor milein
  • 35.0 se upar — Obese Class II+: Fori medical attention chahiye
⚠️ Pakistani aur Asian logon ke liye khas baat: WHO kehta hai ke Asian logon mein 23 se upar BMI bhi overweight consider hota hai — kyunki hamare body fat distribution alag hoti hai. Agar aapka BMI 23-25 ke darmiyan hai to bhi diet ka khayal rakhein.

🧮 BMI Formula — Kaise Calculate Hota Hai

BMI calculate karna bahut aasaan hai. Metric system mein: apna weight kilogram mein lein, aur height meter mein convert karein (cm ko 100 se divide karein). Phir weight ko height ke square se divide karein. Misaalan: 65kg weight, 165cm height = 65 ÷ (1.65 × 1.65) = 65 ÷ 2.72 = 23.9 BMI. Hamare tool mein yeh sab automatic hai!

❓ Aksar Pooche Jane Wale Sawal — FAQ

Q: BMI kya hota hai aur kyun important hai?
A: BMI yani Body Mass Index aapke weight aur height ke ratio se bana ek number hai. Yeh batata hai ke aap healthy weight mein hain ya nahi. Doctors is number se quickly assess karte hain ke kisi ko weight-related health risks hain ya nahi — jaise diabetes, heart disease, ya high blood pressure.
Q: Healthy BMI kitna hona chahiye?
A: 18.5 se 24.9 ke darmiyan BMI normal aur healthy maana jata hai. Pakistani aur Asian logon ke liye 23 se upar bhi overweight consider hota hai. 25 se 29.9 overweight, aur 30 se upar obese hai.
Q: BMI calculator Pakistan mein kaise use karein?
A: Upar box mein apna weight kg mein aur height cm mein daalo, phir "Calculate" button dabao. Foran aapka BMI number, category (Urdu mein), ideal weight range aur visual scale aa jayega.
Q: Agar BMI high ho to kya karein?
A: Ghabrao mat — yeh fix ho sakta hai. Pehle apne doctor ya nutritionist se milein. Roz 30 minute walking shuru karo, fried food aur cold drinks kam karo, pani zyada piyo. Dheere dheere weight kam hoga. Crash dieting se bachein — yeh harmful hoti hai.
Q: Kya BMI 100% accurate hota hai?
A: BMI ek general indicator hai — yeh muscle mass, bone density, gender, aur age ka alag se hisaab nahi karta. Ek bodybuilder ka BMI high ho sakta hai lekin woh perfectly healthy ho. Isliye BMI ko ek guide ki tarah use karo, final diagnosis ke liye doctor se milein.
Q: Bacchon ka BMI kaise check karein?
A: Bacchon ka BMI adults se alag calculate hota hai — unka BMI age aur gender ke hisaab se interpret hota hai. Hamare tool mein standard adult BMI calculate hota hai. Bacchon ke liye unke paediatrician se milein.
Q: BMI Prime kya hota hai?
A: BMI Prime aapke BMI ko 25 (upper limit of normal) se divide karka milta hai. Agar BMI Prime 1.0 se kam hai toh normal, 1.0 se upar overweight. Yeh normal BMI range ke comparison mein ek simple ratio hai.

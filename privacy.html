4. Cookies

Hamari site cookies use karti hai better user experience ke liye. Cookies small text files hoti hain jo aapke browser mein store hoti hain. Yeh cookies Google AdSense aur Google Analytics ke liye use hoti hain.

Aap apne browser settings mein cookies disable kar sakte hain, lekin isse site ki kuch features properly kaam nahi kar sakti.

Kai baar hamen exact age ki zaroorat hoti hai — government job form fill karte waqt, PPSC/FPSC apply karte waqt, passport banwate waqt, ya sirf curiosity se. Hamare tool mein bas apni DOB daalo aur ek click mein poori detail aa jayegi — koi calculation nahi, koi mehnat nahi.

❓ Aksar Pooche Jane Wale Sawal — FAQ

Q: Age calculator Pakistan mein kaise use karein?
A: Upar diye gaye box mein apni date of birth select karo aur "Calculate" button dabao. Foran result aa jayega — years, months, days, hours aur seconds sab mein. Koi extra step nahi.
Q: Job form ke liye exact age kaise nikalen?
A: "Kis date tak calculate karein" wale box mein job ki closing date daalo — us din tak tumhari exact age calculate ho jayegi. Yeh PPSC, FPSC, NTS aur sarkari naukri forms ke liye perfect hai.
Q: Kya yeh age calculator bilkul free hai?
A: Haan, 100% free hai. Koi account banana nahi, koi payment nahi, koi limit nahi. ToolsUrdu ke sab tools hamesha free hain.
Q: Kya main kisi aur ki age calculate kar sakta hoon?
A: Bilkul! Kisi bhi shakhs ki date of birth daalo — unki exact age calculate ho jayegi. Bacche, parents, dost — koi bhi.
Q: Age seconds mein kaise calculate hoti hai?
A: Hamare tool mein paidaish ki date se aaj tak ke total milliseconds calculate hote hain, phir unhe seconds mein convert kiya jata hai. Ek saal mein approximately 3 crore 15 lakh seconds hote hain!
Q: Zodiac sign kya hoti hai aur kaise pata chalta hai?
A: Zodiac sign (burj) aapki paidaish ki date ke hisaab se decide hoti hai. 12 burj hain — Aries se Pisces tak. Hamare tool mein automatically aapki DOB ke hisaab se zodiac sign show hoti hai.
Q: Kya aapka tool accurate hai?
A: Haan, hamare tool mein JavaScript ki built-in Date functions use hoti hain jo leap years aur exact days bhi count karte hain. Result 100% accurate hota hai.

❓ Aksar Pooche Jane Wale Sawal — FAQ

Q: Case converter kya hota hai aur kyun use karte hain?
A: Case converter ek online tool hai jo text ke letters ka case badal deta hai — jaise sab ko capital banana ya sab ko small karna. Jab kisi aur jagah se text copy karo aur format galat ho, ya jab heading ya title ka case theek karna ho, tab yeh tool bohot kaam aata hai.
Q: Title Case aur Sentence Case mein kya farq hai?
A: Title Case mein har lafz ka pehla letter capital hota hai — jaise "Hello World Pakistan". Sentence Case mein sirf poore sentence ka pehla letter capital hota hai — jaise "Hello world pakistan". Title Case zyada tar headings ke liye use hota hai, Sentence Case normal writing ke liye.
Q: Kya Urdu text bhi convert hoga?
A: Urdu script (nastaliq) mein uppercase aur lowercase ka concept nahi hota, isliye Urdu text convert nahi hoga. Lekin Roman Urdu (English haroof mein likhi Urdu) aur English text fully convert hota hai. Mixed text bhi kaam karta hai — sirf English/Roman part convert hoga.
Q: Kya yeh tool bilkul free hai?
A: Haan, bilkul free hai. Koi account banana nahi, koi payment nahi, koi limit nahi. ToolsUrdu ke sab tools hamesha free hain.
Q: Mera text safe hai? Koi server pe jata hai kya?
A: Bilkul safe hai. Aapka text sirf aapke browser mein process hota hai — koi data hamare ya kisi bhi server pe nahi jata. Aapka confidential ya personal text completely private rehta hai.
Q: Kitna text paste kar sakte hain?
A: Koi limit nahi — chota sentence ho ya poora article, sab convert ho jata hai. Tool browser mein run hota hai toh practically unlimited text handle kar sakta hai.
Q: Alternate Case aur Inverse Case ka kya use hai?
A: Alternate Case (hElLo WoRlD) zyada tar fun, memes, ya social media pe stylish posts ke liye use hota hai. Inverse Case (hELLO wORLD) tab use hota hai jab aapko CAPS LOCK galti se on tha aur text ulta likh gaya — yeh ek click mein sahi kar deta hai.
